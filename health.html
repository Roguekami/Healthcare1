About us

Meet Our Founder

As a registered nurse with over 12 years of experience in nursing service, I am deeply committed to serving humanity through compassionate care and unwavering dedication. My journey in nursing has been shaped by a desire to make a difference in the lives of others, and my experiences in both civilian and military healthcare settings have reinforced my commitment to this noble calling.

A Commitment to Service

Throughout my career, I have had the privilege of serving individuals and families in diverse healthcare settings, from hospital wards to military deployments. Each experience has strengthened my resolve to provide the highest quality of care and support to those in need. As a member of the military, I have witnessed firsthand the profound impact that dedicated healthcare professionals can have on the lives of others, and it is this spirit of service that drives everything we do at Kays Healthcare Nursing Agency.

Our Mission

At Kays Healthcare Nursing Agency, our mission is simple yet powerful: to extend the same level of care and compassion that I have dedicated my career to providing to those in our community who need it most. We are committed to delivering safe, cost-effective, and personalized healthcare services that promote positive client outcomes and enhance quality of life. Our services are provided without regard to religion, age, race, sex, national origin, or disability, ensuring that everyone receives the care and support they deserve.

Our services

Healthcare Staffing Agency

We help healthcare facilities find the right people for the job. Whether it's nurses, medical assistants, or other healthcare professionals, we make sure you have the support you need to keep things running smoothly.

In-Home Care Services

We offer personalized care and support for those who want to stay at home but need a little extra help. Our caregivers provide assistance with daily tasks, medication management, and companionship to help clients feel comfortable and safe in their own homes.

Nursing referrals service Agency

Need a nurse? We've got you covered. Whether it's for a short-term assignment or a long-term placement, we connect healthcare facilities with qualified nurses who are ready to step in and provide top-notch care

Policy and Procedure Development

We help healthcare agencies create clear and easy-to-follow guidelines for how things should be done. From standard operating procedures to safety protocols, we make sure everyone knows what to do to keep things running smoothly.
